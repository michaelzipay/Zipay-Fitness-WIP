Zipay Fitness

Zipay Fitness is a personal training service prioritizing healthy lifestyles for college students at the University of Scranton.

A subscription with Zipay Fitness includes:

  • Preliminary consultation via Google Forms
  • A personalized training plan based on your conultation
  • A gym visit together to demonstrate each exercise and asses your favorites
  • A final, revised plan based on the gym visit

About The Owner

Michael Zipay is a dedicated personal trainer with over six years of consistent lifting experience and a deep passion for strength training. His journey began with a personal commitment to fitness, which quickly evolved into a desire to help others achieve their goals. Over the years, Michael has guided numerous clients—ranging from complete beginners to intermediate lifters—through the fundamentals of weightlifting, helping them build confidence, strength, and sustainable habits. With a focus on proper form, injury prevention, and personalized programming, Michael brings both expertise and encouragement to every session, making fitness accessible and rewarding for everyone he trains.
