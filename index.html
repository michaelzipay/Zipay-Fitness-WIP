Zipay Fitness

Zipay Fitness is a personal training service prioritizing healthy lifestyles for college students at the University of Scranton.

A subscription with Zipay Fitness includes:

  • Preliminary consultation via Google Forms
  • A personalized training plan based on your conultation
  • A gym visit together to demonstrate each exercise and asses your favorites
  • A final, revised plan based on the gym visit
