Zipay Fitness is the Passion Project of Michael Zipay

Michael created Zipay Fitness in order to help with the less enjoyable parts of fitness. New lifters often quit lifting because they don't enjoy it. This reason is not "I don't want to get fit anymore." Zipay Fitness exists to solve this problem. With Zipay Fitness, your training program will be uniquely yours, curated based on your likes and dislikes. These will be assessed based on a preliminary gym visit with the intention of trying the exercises you will be doing in the future. Ready to make the switch and get fit with Zip?
